Sharjah International Book Fair

Date: 22 October 2024

The Sharjah International Book Fair (SIBF) is one of the world's premier literary events, held annually in Sharjah. It offers not only a general reading, literary, cultural exchange, and support for education, but also a wide range of cultural events, including book signings, panel discussions, and lectures. The event features local and international authors, publishers, and literary professionals, making it a must-attend event for book lovers.

UAE Book Expo

Date: 10 November 2024

The UAE Book Expo is a major event that includes book exhibits, author signings, and other book-related activities. Sibneel Annual Book Fair

Date: 15 December 2024

The Sibneel Annual Book Fair is a major cultural event dedicated to books and reading. It typically spreads over several days and features a wide selection of books, from bestsellers to niche genres.
